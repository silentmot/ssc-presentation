Core Feature Showdown

SIRC (SGS) Platform - The Legacy Approach

  • User Dashboards: No dashboards; manual reports or raw data exports.
  • Access Control: Static, limited role separation.
  • Inventory Management: Manual, error-prone tracking.
  • Client Wallets: No integrated wallets; external/manual payments.
  • Automation: Requires manual triggers for device integrations.
  • Workflow Customization: Rigid workflows, manual workarounds for changes.
  • System Integration: Data siloed by site/device.
  • Reporting/Analytics: Manual reports, basic analytics.
  • Shift Management: Offline shift management.
  • Rejection Handling: Manual process, poor traceability.

SCC (SWMS) Platform - The Modern Solution

  • User Dashboards: Advanced, real-time, customizable dashboards.
  • Access Control: Robust multi-level access model.
  • Inventory Management: Real-time, automated tracking.
  • Client Wallets: Built-in digital wallets, automated flows.
  • Automation: End-to-end process automation.
  • Workflow Customization: Highly configurable workflows.
  • System Integration: Centralized data platform, real-time integration.
  • Reporting/Analytics: Powerful, on-demand reporting, full audit trails.
  • Shift Management: Integrated scheduling, live dashboards.
  • Rejection Handling: Systematic, transparent, electronic approvals.

Financial Insights & Cost Management

SGS: Financial Overview

  • Annual Subscription Cost: SAR 1.5 million/year for software usage (recurring, external vendor-controlled).
  • Financial Risks:
    • High ongoing costs.
    • Inflexible pricing.
    • Potential for unexpected vendor-driven increases.
  • Limited Cost Control:
    • No ownership of core platform.
    • No opportunity for customization to reduce operational costs.

SWMS: Financial Advantages

  • Self-Hosted Solution: One-time infrastructure and deployment cost, with ongoing operational expenses controlled in-house.
  • Cost Savings:
    • Eliminates recurring licensing fees (saves SAR 1.5 million/year as a baseline).
    • Reduces operational overhead through automation (fewer staff hours needed for manual data entry and reconciliation).
    • Scalable without third-party pricing penalties; adding new sites or users does not escalate software costs.
  • Financial Visibility: Real-time revenue, expense, and operational cost tracking for each site and the organization as a whole.
